2 Formula — real USDA base price × score multipliers
Stage Base Price × Grade Premium(G) × Quality(H,C) × Sustainability(S)
Stage Base Price = real USDA / Texas auction price for that weight class (e.g., 550 lb steers at $/cwt).
Scores are converted into three multipliers:
Grade Premium(G) — better genetics → more Prime/Choice.
Quality(H,C) — health + certifications → fewer losses and program premiums.
Sustainability(S) — lower methane + better USRSB indicators → modest “green” uplift.
Typical combined range is 0.90–1.30×. Only high-scoring cattle are listed, so most animals trade above the local sale barn.
